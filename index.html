Introduction

Engineer, maker, climber, early-start enthusiast.

I was born in Australia but spent most of my childhood growing up in Singapore. After high school, I worked as a Manufacturing Technician in Thailand before returning to Sydney to complete my Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering degree at UNSW in 2025.

Today, I work as a Prototyping Engineer at KeenzKustoms, where I get to turn ideas into real products through design, prototyping, testing, and problem solving.

Outside of engineering, you'll usually find me at a climbing gym, hiking with my girlfriend, or trying to convince my friends to join me for an early morning adventure. If you're into bouldering, feel free to reach out. I'm always looking for climbing buddies.
