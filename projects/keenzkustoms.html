Project

Custom Product Development – KeenzKustoms

Project Overview

Customer-focused engineering solutions developed through CAD design, rapid prototyping, fabrication-aware iteration, and practical testing.

Engineering Tools Used

CAD designRapid prototypingFabricationProduct testing

Problem

Custom products often begin as open-ended customer needs. The engineering challenge is to define requirements, constraints, materials, and a build path quickly.

Approach

Translate the customer goal into CAD, create prototypes, test fit/function, gather feedback, and refine the design until it works in the real context.

Challenges

Balancing speed, manufacturability, cost, durability, and customer expectations while still leaving room for iteration.

Results

Delivered practical custom solutions and strengthened a product development workflow that moves smoothly from idea to prototype to finished part.

Image Gallery

Key Takeaways

The best prototype is not always the prettiest first model. It is the one that answers the next most important question and moves the product closer to something people can use.
