Project

Gearbox Design Project

Project Overview

A mechanical design project combining CAD modelling, engineering calculations, FEA analysis, and design optimisation for gearbox components.

Engineering Tools Used

CAD modellingEngineering calculationsFEADesign optimisation

Problem

Gearbox design requires aligning geometry, loads, stress, manufacturability, and assembly constraints so the design performs reliably.

Approach

Created CAD models, performed calculations for key design parameters, used FEA to assess stress behaviour, and iterated toward a stronger design.

Challenges

Connecting simplified engineering calculations with detailed geometry and simulation results without losing sight of practical manufacturing constraints.

Results

Produced an optimised design package supported by calculations, simulation insight, and design reasoning.

Image Gallery

Key Takeaways

Strong mechanical design is a conversation between calculations, geometry, simulation, and judgement. Each method catches something the others can miss.
